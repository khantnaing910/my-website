Common Mistakes and Corrections
1. Incorrect Order of Filling:
Mistake: Not following the correct order of orbital lling.
Correction: Use the Aufbau principle and refer to an energy level diagram.
2. Ignoring Hund's Rule:
Mistake: Pairing electrons in degenerate orbitals before all orbitals are singly occupied.
Correction: Ensure that electrons occupy all degenerate orbitals singly before pairing.
3. Miscounting Electrons:
Mistake: Incorrectly counting the number of electrons, leading to incorrect congurations.
Correction: Carefully count the total number of electrons and distribute them according to
the principles.
4. Incorrect Use of Noble Gas Notation:
Mistake: Using the wrong noble gas or not including all remaining electrons.
Correction: Ensure the correct noble gas is used and add all subsequent electrons
accurately.
